Imagine you're in Paris, and you're tasked with painting a wall in a room. The wall has an area of 250 square meters. You decide to paint the wall using a roller brush. The rate at which you apply the paint is given by the function f(x)=5x^2 square meters per hour, where x represents the time in hours.

a) Determine the total amount of paint, in square meters, applied to the wall during the first 3 hours.

b) Calculate the integral ∫ 0 → 3 (5x^2) dx to find the total amount of paint applied to the wall during the first 3 hours.
Mathy Co
Think about what the function f(x) =5x^2 represents. How would you interpret f(x) in the context of painting the wall? Then, considering that the rate of applying paint is given by this function, how could you calculate the total paint applied over the first 3 hours?
